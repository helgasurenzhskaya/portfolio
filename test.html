about me

As one said, "designer is a creator of meaning and form" — and that's exactly who I am.

I help brands to embody strong visual communications, consistent interfaces and efficient user experiences.

What's so special in my designs?
The answer is simple — I develop thoughtful concepts in order to unveil the story of the project. It is a problem-solving process which transforms the project's attitude into the hewn, visual form of its essence.

Also, I'm an aspiring ARTivist, who believes that modern technologies and digital art can bring profound changes to the world. That's why I am passionate about my work.
