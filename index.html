about me

I'm Helga Surenzhskaya, Ukrainian UX/UI and graphic designer.

I base my work on key principles of minimalism and usability. But I always apply a special approach to creating every new design, in order to revive the story behind particular project.

What's so special in my designs?
The answer is simple - I love experimenting with various methods of merging layers. Which means I create unique connections between form and color.

Also, I'm an aspiring developer, who believes that modern technologies can make this world a better place for everyone to live. That's why I would like to work in the IT field.

Get in touch, let's make something awesome together!
